Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 29, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Portify ("we", "our", or "the extension") handles your information when you use our Chrome Extension and web application available at portify.gr.

1. Information We Do NOT Collect

Portify does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Your name, email address, or any identifying information
  • Your browsing history or visited URLs
  • Your bookmarks content (stored locally only)
  • Your IP address or location data
  • Any usage analytics or behavioral data

2. Data Storage

All data created by Portify (bookmarks, preferences, recent visits) is stored exclusively on your device using:

  • Chrome Extension: chrome.storage.sync or chrome.storage.local — data stays within your Chrome profile and is never transmitted to our servers.
  • Web App: localStorage in your browser — data never leaves your device.

We have no servers and no databases. We cannot access your data.

3. Permissions Used

The Portify Chrome Extension requests the following permission:

  • storage — used solely to save your bookmarks and preferences locally on your device.

No other permissions are requested or required.

4. Third-Party Services

Portify uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Favicon Service (google.com/s2/favicons) — to display website icons. This is a standard public API and does not involve personal data.
  • Google Fonts — to load fonts for the web app interface.

The web version of Portify (portify.gr) uses Google Analytics to collect anonymous, aggregated usage statistics (page views, session duration). This data cannot be used to identify individual users. The Chrome Extension does not include Google Analytics.

5. No Account Required

Portify does not require you to create an account, provide an email address, or authenticate in any way. The product is fully functional without any registration.

6. Children's Privacy

Portify does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. The extension contains no age-restricted content.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of Portify after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
